YULA

100% Natural Energy Drink
Inspired by the Amazon Rain Forest

About Us

We’re determined that YULA has maximum uplift for you, minimum impact for the planet. This means more to us than sticking ‘organic’ on a can and thinking we’re done. That’s why we’ve committed to planting 250,000 trees in the Amazon by 2021, through our partnership with reforestation non-profit One Tree Planted.

Inspired by the raw energy of the Amazon rainforest, we’ve combined green maté with green tea and guarana to create an energy drink that hits the spot. We’ve worked really hard to find ingredients and packaging that meet our ethical standards. We’re proud to say YULA is made with organic ingredients, all our plant-based ingredients are sustainably sourced and our green tea is certified by the Rainforest Alliance. Plus our cans are 100% recyclable.

Why Drink YULA

This life-giving force is at the heart of what we do. Whether it’s a long day, tough workout or 3pm slump, sometimes we just need a lift. So we created YULA - made with organic and sustainably sourced ingredients, and 100% natural energy. Just 9 ingredients including sparkling water, botanical extracts and fruit juices, without taurine and low in sugar. A drink to unleash your inner spirit and restore your mind, body and soul.

A place of breath-taking natural beauty, our reforestation project is located in a zone of the Amazon that has been ravaged by slash and burn agriculture. Deforestation here has had a devasting impact on the environment, the wildlife and the local people but we’re determined to make a real difference. Our tree planting programme will restore 212 hectares of rainforest, offset 5443 tons of CO2 and support 342 farming families. The project is located in the buffer zone between Tambopata National Reserve, Bahuaja-Sonene National Park, and the city of Puerto Maldonado. It is home to some incredible species that have disappeared in other places. Rebuilding the rainforest here will help conserve the habitat for more than 30 high-conservation value species.
